# AuréLéa Brand DNA

AuréLéa represents quiet, refined elegance in movement. The visual identity should feel calm, minimal, feminine, and premium, inspired by Pilates studios, natural light, and Mediterranean softness.

The design language favors clean compositions, soft neutral tones, warm lighting, and an atmosphere of balance and serenity. Products should appear sculptural and tactile, as if placed in a peaceful studio environment rather than a commercial catalog setting.

## Visual style guidelines

- Minimal, uncluttered compositions
- Soft natural lighting with warm highlights
- Calm color palette with sand, cream, peach, muted blues, olive, and cocoa tones
- Premium textures such as knit fabric, matte rubber, soft textiles, and natural materials
- Elegant negative space
- Subtle shadows and gentle gradients

## Avoid

- Harsh lighting or strong contrast
- Overly saturated colors
- Busy scenes or excessive props
- Cheap commercial stock-photo aesthetics

The overall feeling should communicate: **quiet luxury, balance, and elegance through movement.**

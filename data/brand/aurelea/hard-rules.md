# AuréLéa Global Hard Rules

These rules are non-negotiable and must be enforced in every generated image.

- All images are intended for Amazon product listing usage and must meet e-commerce quality standards.
- The AuréLéa brand identity must remain elegant, minimal, feminine, and premium at all times.
- No harsh lighting, overly saturated colors, or cheap commercial stock-photo aesthetics.
- Products must never be visually distorted, misrepresented, or obscured.
- No text, watermarks, or logos should appear in the generated image unless explicitly requested.
- The overall mood must communicate quiet luxury, balance, and elegance through movement.

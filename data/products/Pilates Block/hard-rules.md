# Pilates Block – Hard Rules

Product identity
- The size of the Pilates Block Ball is 4 * 6 * 9 inch with an embossed AuréLéa logo in the bottom center.

Logo and branding
- The word "AuréLéa" must appear embossed in the bottom center of the block.

Logo typography
- The word "AuréLéa" must appear in the same typography as in the reference images.
- The logo uses the font family GFS Didot.
